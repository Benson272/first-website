Learn how to make a Cheesecake!

Do you enjoy the savory flavor of the oh-so-tasty cheesecake but never have the money to pay for them. Well soon you'll be able to make your own cheesecake at home for a fraction of the price! Making a cheesecake at home is actually quite simple. You don't need to be a skilled baker. All you need are the ingredients and determination. I have always loved the taste of cheesecake but could never afford any. Now that I know how to make one, I don't have to pay for the overpriced ones at the store.

All you need are:

  • 15 crushed graham crackers
  • 2 tablespoons of melted butter
  • 32 ounces of cream cheese
  • 1 1/2 cups of sugar
  • 3/4 cups of milk
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 cup of sour cream
  • 1 tablespoon of vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup of all-purpose flour

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9 inch springform pan.
  2. In a medium bowl, mix graham cracker crumbs with melted butter. Press onto bottom of springform pan.
  3. In a large bowl, mix cream cheese with sugar until smooth. Blend in milk, and then mix in the eggs one at a time, mixing just enough to incorporate. Mix in sour cream, vanilla and flour until smooth. Pour filling into prepared crust.
  4. Bake in preheated oven for 1 hour. Turn the oven off, and let cake cool in oven with the door closed for 5 to 6 hours; this prevents cracking. Chill in refrigerator until serving.
